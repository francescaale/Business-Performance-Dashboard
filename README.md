# 📊 Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The objective was to analyze an online retail sales dataset to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, customer purchasing patterns, and country-level sales performance. An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to present key business insights and actionable recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Clean and prepare raw sales data
- Analyze business performance using key KPIs
- Identify revenue trends over time
- Discover top-selling products
- Evaluate sales by country
- Identify high-value customers
- Present findings through an interactive dashboard

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Microsoft Excel/CSV
- GitHub

---

## 📂 Dataset

**Dataset:** Online Retail Dataset

The dataset contains historical online retail transactions, including:

- Invoice Number
- Product Description
- Quantity Sold
- Unit Price
- Invoice Date
- Customer ID
- Country

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Quantity Sold
- Average Order Value
- Total Customers

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- Revenue Over Time
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Top 5 Products by Revenue
- Revenue by Country
- Quantity Sold by Product
- Top 5 Customers by Revenue
- Revenue vs Quantity Scatter Plot
- Interactive Slicers (Country, Year, Month)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- The United Kingdom generated over **£8.2 million** in revenue, significantly outperforming all other countries.
- **DOTCOM POSTAGE** was the highest revenue-generating product.
- Revenue peaked during **November and December**, indicating strong seasonal demand.
- A small group of customers contributed a large share of total revenue.
- Several products achieved high sales volumes but generated comparatively lower revenue due to lower unit prices.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase marketing efforts for high-performing products.
- Prioritize retention strategies for high-value customers.
- Increase inventory before peak sales periods.
- Expand marketing in countries with strong revenue potential.
- Bundle low-cost, high-volume products to improve average order value.
